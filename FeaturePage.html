Professional Elective

Technology has made many amazing things become a standard in the daily lives of the people in 2023. Movies, posters, music and digital art are a great source of information and entertainemnt; all of this is presented in a digestible medium teeming with craftsmanship and artistry.

With Professional Elective, you too can be part of a community that that creates professional grade art of your choosing. Whether your interest lies in visual art of music, it's got you covered. In this subject you will learn how to utilize programs like Adobe Photoshop, Capcut, Davinci Resolve, Audacity, Canva and many more.

Other Subjects

InMana

Introductory management

Introductory management is a subject that tackles the creation, manipulation and using of digital databases. The most important topic of this subject is the SQL language; armed with the knowledge on this language, on is able to create a database with different tables and retrieve data down to specific details. Using programs like MSSQL and/or MySQL, this subject will get you started on your journey on becoming a master of SQL and digital databases.
